About development cashflow

Development projects typically front-load costs (land, consultants, DA) in months 1–6, with the bulk of construction spend in the middle phase. Revenue is recognised only at settlement — often 12–18 months after construction begins. This cashflow profile is why development finance is expensive: the lender carries the risk for an extended period before drawdown is repaid.

Disclaimer: This tool produces indicative feasibility estimates only. Development projects involve complex variables including planning risk, construction cost escalation, market movements, and finance conditions not fully captured here. Engage a qualified development manager, quantity surveyor, and legal adviser before committing to any development. GST calculations are simplified — consult a tax adviser for margin scheme eligibility. All amounts in AUD.
